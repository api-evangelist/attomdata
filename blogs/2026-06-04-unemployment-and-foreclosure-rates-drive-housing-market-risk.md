ATTOM's Q1 2026 Housing Impact Report identifies the most vulnerable housing markets based on unemployment, foreclosure rates, affordability, and equity measures. Florida and California contained the highest concentration of at-risk counties, while Tennessee boasted some of the safest markets in the nation. The greatest risk remains in counties where unemployment rates are above 5 percent and homes are being foreclosed at greater rates.
